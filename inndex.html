Scanopy

The English OCR app with Malayalam translation aims to provide users with a convenient and reliable tool for extracting text from images, documents, or any visual content in English. The app utilizes advanced image processing techniques and machine learning algorithms to recognize and convert the English text into editable and searchable format. The core functionality of the app revolves around the OCR engine, which analyses the input image and identifies individual characters or words. It employs a combination of optical and linguistic analysis to accurately recognize the English text. The OCR engine is trained on large datasets to improve its accuracy and handle various fonts, styles, and image qualities
